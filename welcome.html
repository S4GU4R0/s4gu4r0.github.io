Welcome to the Terminal

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

This is the first post. A test. A beginning.

I've been thinking about what it means to build something in public, to write without knowing who's reading, to create a space that exists both as a record and as a living document.

The terminal aesthetic feels right for this. It's minimal, functional, honest. No hiding behind design. Just text, links, thoughts.

This site follows the rule of least power: XHTML for structure, CSS for presentation, no JavaScript unless absolutely necessary. Everything degrades gracefully. Everything works without bells and whistles.

More to come. For now, this is enough.
